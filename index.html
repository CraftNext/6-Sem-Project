Frequently Asked Questions

How much is shipping?

Flat ₹49 per order, or free on orders over ₹999.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery?

Yes — pay when your order arrives at your doorstep, no online payment required.

Can I cancel an order after placing it?

Yes, as long as it's still marked "pending" in My Orders. Once a seller confirms or ships it, contact us and we'll do what we can.

What if an item arrives damaged?

Since most items are handmade in limited quantities, please check photos and descriptions carefully before ordering. If something arrives damaged or not as described, contact us within 48 hours with photos and we'll work it out with the seller directly.
