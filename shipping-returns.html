Shipping & Returns

Every item is handmade to order or shipped from an individual artisan's shop, so timelines can vary a little between sellers.

Shipping

  • Flat shipping fee of ₹49 per order.
  • Free shipping on orders over ₹999.
  • Payment is Cash on Delivery — you pay when your order arrives.

Order cancellation

You can cancel an order yourself from My Orders as long as it's still marked "pending". Once a seller confirms or ships it, the item is already being prepared — contact us and we'll do what we can.

Returns

Because most items are handmade in limited quantities, we ask that you check the photos and description carefully before ordering. If an item arrives damaged or clearly not as described, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos and we'll work it out with the seller directly.
