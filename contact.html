Common questions

How do I check my order status?

Sign in and go to My Account → My Orders. Guest orders don't have an account to check back on, but you'll get the order ID at checkout.

Can I cancel an order?

Yes — from My Orders, any order still marked "pending" can be cancelled. Once a seller marks it confirmed or shipped, contact us directly.

What payment methods are supported?

Cash on Delivery only, for now.

I'm a seller — how do orders reach me?

Check the Orders tab in your Seller Dashboard.
