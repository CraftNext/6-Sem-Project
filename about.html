About CraftNext

A small marketplace built around one idea: handmade shouldn't be hard to find.

CraftNext connects independent artisans — starting with traditional Gujarati craft forms like Lippan art, mandala painting, and diya work — directly with buyers who want something made by hand, not mass-produced.

How it works

  • Sellers list their own work, set their own prices, and manage their own orders.
  • Buyers pay Cash on Delivery for now — see our shipping & returns policy for details.
  • Every seller's shop page shows their location and contact details, so you know who made what you're buying.

Selling on CraftNext

If you make things by hand and want to sell them, start here. Listing is free — we don't take a cut of your sales.
