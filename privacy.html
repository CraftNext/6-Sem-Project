Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026. Plain-language summary of what we collect and why.

What we collect

  • Account info: name, email, and password (hashed, never stored in plain text) when you register.
  • Order info: shipping name, address, phone, and email — needed to actually deliver what you ordered. Guest checkout stores the same fields without creating an account.
  • Seller info: shop name, location, and phone number, shown publicly on your shop page so buyers know who they're buying from.
  • Cart & wishlist: stored only in your browser's local storage, not on our servers, until you check out.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell or share your data with third parties for advertising.
  • We don't use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.
  • We never see or store your payment details — checkout is Cash on Delivery.

How we use email

We email you a one-time code to verify your account, and a reset link if you request a password reset. That's it — no marketing email unless you explicitly subscribe via our newsletter, which you can unsubscribe from at any time.

Your data, your control

You can update or remove your profile information at any time from My Account. To fully delete your account, contact us.
